About Us

A vibrant and dynamic wholly Zimbabwean company, Horizonview Technologies (Pvt) Ltd as the name connotes, entered the ICT market space with a vision and promise beyond what the naked eye can behold - a journey to the Horizon and beyond.

With none of the standard rhetoric and often over-used statements of being the biggest and best players in the ICT field that most established players claim, Horizonview Technologies derives its energy and motivation from the drive to offer best-fit tailor-made ICT Solutions to its chosen market segment.

Thus the focus at Horizonview Technologies is not on self aggrandisement but instead lies within the fulfilment of its customers' specific expectations and requirements.
